Young Players

Helping your child reach new heights, both on and Off The Court

With our platform, you can book mentoring or training sessions with some of your favorite players.

Learn from the best

Athletes at the top of their game all have something in common; support and guidance from people who inspire them. Off The Court gives you access to successful high-level athletes through video calls, messaging, and in-person workouts.

Aligned goals

Every mentorship is a unique and personal experience. Establishing a game plan and aligning goals is an important first step of every program. We provide direct communication channels for both mentees and parents, as well as a personal dashboard to track activity and progress.

Improved wellbeing

Our mission is to help young athletes gain extra knowledge and performance training from elite athletes. Our mentors have been vetted and have access to a wide range of mentoring best practices. This will translate to higher performance for your kids, as well as an improved overall wellbeing.
