Off The Court

Our focus is to help the current generation of athletes mentor the next. We believe that winning in youth athletics is prioritized more than a player's wellbeing. With our platform, current NCAA and Pro athletes will help your kids not only perform better, but also become a more well-rounded individual.

By Athletes, For Athletes

Our mission is important to me as a former athlete. Having a good mentor changed the course of my career and I believe that building these personal relationships will help younger players reach their full potential.

Off The Court seeks to help the next generations of players, but we also believe in the value of being a mentor. Current NCAA and pro athletes can build a side business while working with younger players to make a positive impact in their community.

Bill Amis
Cofounder and CEO of OTC
University of Hawai'i Basketball - '11
