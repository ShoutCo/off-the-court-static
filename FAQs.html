FAQs for Parents   

Who determines the price?

Our mentors/coaches determine their prices and services offered.

How are parents involved?

We encourage parents to be engaged and involved, but it is also important for children to retain ownership of the process. You can always contact your mentor directly through the OTC dashboard or email us at contact@offthecourt.co. We will also be sharing newsletters, blogs, and best practice advise to help you support your child’s journey in sports.

Is it safe?

Safety is our main priority. As a parent you have full oversight into everything your child sees and does on our platform. This includes a view of messages to and from their coach/mentor, as well as your own separate coach/mentor messaging channel. In addition to rigorous background checks of all our sellers, and rating and review systems, we have partnered with sports psychologists to give our mentor/coaches access to the latest best practices for mentoring your child. Should you have questions you can always reach us directly by emailing contact@offthecourt.co.

FAQs for Mentor/Coaches

What if I’ve never been a mentor?

You will go through an onboarding process that has been designed to help you build a strong and effective mentorship relationship with your young athletes. We will have a library of resources to reference, and we will have our sports psychologists ready if you ever have questions or need extra resources. Treat this as an opportunity to be a positive role model in a young athlete’s life and you will grow together.

Where should I hold the personal training sessions?

It will be up to you to find the location for personal training based on the type of session. It can be in a field, a spare gym, or possibly at your client’s home. Speak with your university compliance officer regarding using university facilities before meeting a client on campus.

Where do I hold the virtual meetings?

All communication should be conducted through the OTC dashboard. This will keep a level of separation between the young athlete and your personal life. You can hold virtual meetings as well as conduct messaging between your young athlete or their parent through the OTC dashboard.

How do clients find me?

High level athletes can list themselves and their services on the OTC marketplace. Clients can filter their search by location, sport, school, service, or directly by athlete’s name. Additionally, you can link your storefront in your bio on your social media platforms to extend your reach.

How many hours per week will this take?

This is entirely up to you. You have the freedom to determine your service offering. You can create monthly mentorships that could involve a few video calls per month as well as a few in-person training sessions. Or you can sell one-off video calls, game tape reviews, motivational messages, shoutout videos, or training sessions. We have developed the platform in such a way as to maximize the time you have by limiting the administrative work you have to do. For example, if you only have 3-4 hours a week, we can help you create a program to have 3-4 mentees/young players to maximize your reach.

How much money can I make?

This is also entirely up to you. We will provide pricing and service guidance, but you have the freedom to create your own prices and set your own service offering.

How do I receive payment?

We work with a service call Stripe, a leading payment processing company, to handle the platform transactions. As part of your onboarding process, you'll create an account on their platform with your billing details. When a payment is processed, your take of the transaction will be sent directly to your account. Stripe will then deposit the money in your account at set intervals to your bank account. 

Do I need to pay taxes?

Yes, as money made on Off The Court is considered taxable income, just like any other job. A bonus with using Stripe for our American mentors, is an end of year tax document (1099 form) to make your tax returns as easy as possible. We handle sales tax and VAT (for our European customers).

What can I list on my storefront?

As of our launch, you can list mentorships, personal training sessions, video calls, and shout out videos. We are working on creating some other exciting features to be released shortly.

When can I start selling?

We are working on developing our systems and we expect to launch in a few markets in September 2021.
